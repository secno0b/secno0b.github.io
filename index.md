## Real engineering starts where the documentation stops.

I work in cloud security and architecture, dealing with systems that have to work under real constraints.

Most of what I deal with doesn’t show up in diagrams or vendor docs. It shows up when things don’t behave the way they are supposed to, when integrations don’t support modern auth, and when “best practices” break real systems.

This site is where I write about what actually happens beyond the diagrams.

## What you’ll find here

- Security decisions that don’t fit clean frameworks  
- Azure architecture lessons from production systems  
- Tooling that works and tooling that wastes your time  
- Real-world tradeoffs between control and delivery
